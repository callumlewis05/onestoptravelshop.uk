Cruises

Set sail on the adventure of a lifetime with a cruise holiday that blends relaxation, exploration, and world-class service. Wake up each morning to a new horizon - whether it's the sun-drenched beaches of the Caribbean or the breath taking fjords of Norway, onboard you'll find everything from live entertainment to luxurious spas and exciting activities for all ages, ensuring there's never a dull moment.

Cruises offer the perfect balance of comfort and discovery: unpack once and let your floating resort take you from one stunning destination to the next. Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway, a family-friendly adventure, or a solo escape, there's a cruise to match your style.

Why Choose a Cruise?

See More, Unpack Once

Wake up in a new destination every day without the hassle of constant travel or hotel changes.

Stress-Free Travel

No airport hopping or long road trips. Simply board, relax, and let the ship take you to your destinations.

All-Inclusive Value

Meals, entertainment, and accommodation included - often with extras like drinks or onboard credit.

Perfect for All Travellers

Romantic escapes for couples, family fun with kids, solo travel made easy or luxury for special occasions.

Entertainment for Everyone

Broadway-style shows, kids' clubs, spa treatments, pools and nightlife - something for every age.

Extraordinary Experiences

From Caribbean beaches to European cities to scenic river cruises - cruises give the opportunity for amazing experiences.

Cruise Lines

We only work with the best cruise lines to give you the best holiday possible. Listed below is what some cruise lines offer!
If your cruise line is not listed do not worry as we can still arrange a cruise with a cruise line that is not listed.

MSC Cruises

  • 24 ships in their fleet.

  • Their largest ship MSC World Europa which holds 6762 passengers.

  • Their smallest is MSC Armonia which holds 2620.

  • MSC have three different Experiences on board, Bella, Fantastica and Aurea.

  • You can add on drink packages and extras at time of booking.

  • MSC also offer a private and exclusive section of their ships, this called MSC Yacht Club and customers will receive 24 hour butler and concierge service, an exclusive lounge, restaurant, sundeck and pool, premium drinks and wi-fi and thermal spa access.

  • They offer both fly cruising and UK port sailings.

Marella Cruises

  • British Cruise Line and they are part of TUI.

  • GBP onboard.

  • 5 ships in their fleet.

  • Explorer 2 is an adult only ship.

  • All 5 ships are all-inclusive including drinks as standard, you can upgrade to a premium all-inclusive.

  • Fly Cruise only, from 26 UK airport.

  • You can pick your cabin at an additional cost

  • All ships are freedom dining, You decide when you want to dine

  • They are known for their relaxed, all inclusive style of cruises

Virgin voyages

  • American owned cruise line.

  • USD onboard.

  • 4 ships in their fleet.

  • All of their cruise ships have a capacity of 2770.

  • Adults only (18 years +)

  • Full board cruising, they do not offer drinks packages instead they do a bar tab, this must be pre-booked.

  • The cruise fares include dining at 20+ eateries, soft drinks & water, gratuities, wi-fi and group fitness classes.

  • They are known for their luxury, style and innovative approach to cruising.

Royal Caribbean International

  • An American cruise line.

  • Royal Caribbean have 28 cruise ships.

  • Icon of the Seas is the largest cruise ship in their fleet and in the world holding 7600 passengers at full capacity.

  • Their smallest ship is Grandeur of the seas with a capacity of 2446.

  • They offer mainly fly cruises, but they have one of their ship based in Southampton every summer.

  • Royal Caribbean ships are known for their cutting-edge technology and show-stopping entertainment.

Ambassador Cruise Line

  • British Cruise Line.

  • 3 ships in their fleet.

  • Ambience- 1400 passengers, Ambition- 1200 passengers, Renaissance- 1100 passengers.

  • Uk sailings from 8 ports across the UK, London Tilsbury, Liverpool, Belfast, Dundee, Newcastle Tyne, Bristol Royal Portbury, Falmouth and Portsmouth.

  • Adults only unless they state multi-generation.

  • They offer two fare types Standard and Ambassador (with drinks and gratuities).

Saga Ocean Cruises

  • British Cruise Line.

  • GBP onboard.

  • Ships are adult only (Over 50s).

  • Known for their luxury boutique style ships.

  • Sailings from Dover and Portsmouth.

  • Cruises are all inclusive.

  • Free Chauffeur Service (UK mainland, mileage limit).

  • All drinks included.

  • Gratuities included.

  • Porterage.

  • Balcony Cabin.

  • Wi-fi.

  • A full programme of live music, entertainment and talks.

  • Gym, sauna and Exercise Classes.

  • Afternoon Tea.

  • Free Shuttle Bus at most ports.

  • Excursions at selected ports.
